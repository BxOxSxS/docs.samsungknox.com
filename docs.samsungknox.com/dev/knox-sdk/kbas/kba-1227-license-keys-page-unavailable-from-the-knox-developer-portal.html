License keys page unavailable from the Knox Developer Portal

Last updated March 27th, 2025

Environment

  • Knox SDK
  • Knox Developer Portal

Overview

When looking to generate a Knox development license key, you may notice that the License keys page is unavailable from the Knox Developer Portal.

Cause

To further secure the privacy and data of Galaxy device users, the Samsung Knox team implemented changes to the Knox SDK framework. As a result, we’ve made adjustments to how we distribute developer licenses to our enterprise customers. The License keys page is now only available to approved enterprise customers with valid Samsung Knox use cases.

Workaround

If you require a Knox developer license, you must submit a support ticket and provide a business use case for your app. To do this:

  1. Navigate to the Knox Partner Program Portal, then click Support.

  2. On the Support page, click the Partnership, benefits, and accounts tile, then click SUBMIT A TICKET. The Cases page opens in a new tab.

    If you do not see the Partnership, benefits, and accounts tile, click any of the available tiles that has the support ticket option and click SUBMIT A TICKET.

  3. On the Cases page, click Create a Case.

  4. On the Case Creation dialog, under Support Category, select Knox Partner Program.

  5. Provide your location, then click Next.

  6. In the Subject field, enter “I need access to the Knox Developer Portal License keys page”.

  7. In the Description field, provide the following information:

    • Your contact information:
      • Name
      • Company role
      • Email
      • Phone number
    • Your company information:
      • Company name
      • Knox Partner Program account name
      • Website
      • Company address
    • Your reason for using Knox SDK:
      • Business purpose
      • Use case(s)
      • Whether you use any restricted Knox SDK methods
      • List of the Knox SDK methods you use or are planning to use
    • Your app information:
      • App name
      • App package name
      • App key features
      • Deployment method on devices
        • EMM or side-loaded
      • Deployment environment
        • Cloud or on-premises
      • Link(s) to your app in the Google Play or App Store
    • The name and contact information of your Samsung Knox account representative
