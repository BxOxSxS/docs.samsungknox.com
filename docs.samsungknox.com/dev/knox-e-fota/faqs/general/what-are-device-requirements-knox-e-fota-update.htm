What are the device requirements for a Knox E-FOTA update?

The requirements for a Knox E-FOTA update are the same as those for a standard FOTA update:

  1. Network - the device must have a stable network connection, either Wi-Fi or cellular (with roaming data disabled).
  2. Battery - the battery level of the device must be at 30% or above; the battery must not be removed during the update.
  3. Memory - the device must have at least 500 MB of available storage.
