Frequently Asked Questions (FAQS)

Answers to the most commonly asked questions about KSP

Can I use KSP with an on-premise UEM deployment?

Currently, KSP relies on a Managed Google Play Store for app installation. KSP can't be used in an on-premise deployment unless you allow both your UEM and devices to connect to the Managed Google Play store.
