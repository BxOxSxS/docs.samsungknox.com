View devices

Last updated September 2nd, 2025

After uploading devices to the Reseller Portal, you can view more detailed information about them, such as their upload date, device model, and device status.

Device details

To view device details:

  1. On the Reseller Portal, go to the Customers page.

  2. Under the COMPANY NAME/INTERNAL REFERENCE column, click a customer’s name. The customer details page opens.

  3. Click the DEVICES tab.

    You can sort submitted devices by any of the column headers, or search for devices.

    When searching for devices by IMEI, you can find dual-SIM devices by either of their IMEIs. The device list shows only the primary IMEI (IMEI1), but you can hover over a dual-SIM device to show a tooltip that displays both associated IMEIs.

  4. On the DEVICES tab, click the device that you want to get additional details for.

    • IMEI/MEID: The device’s unique identifier. For dual-SIM devices, both IMEIs are shown under IMEI/MEID.
    • Order Number: The order number that you entered during device upload.
    • Uploaded: The date the device was uploaded.
    • Customer: The customer’s name and Customer ID. The MSP’s name displays if the device is uploaded by a reseller to an MSP.
    • Status: The status of the device. To learn more, see Device status.
    • Model: The model number of the device.
    • Uploaded By: The email of the account that uploaded the device.

Device status

Under the STATUS column of the DEVICES tab, the uploaded device shows one of four device states:

Status Description
Pending The device is successfully uploaded to the customer account, but the customer hasn't approved it yet.
Verified The device is successfully uploaded to the customer account, and the customer has approved it.
MPTS error The device isn't recognized as a valid device from an authorized reseller.
Exchanging The device's IMEI is in the process of exchanging. The device will automatically revert back to it's previous status after completion, regardless of success.
