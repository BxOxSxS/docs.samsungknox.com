Sign up for a partner account

Last updated November 6th, 2025

To enjoy the benefits of the Knox Partner Program, you’ll need to enroll. If you plan to also enroll in the Knox Deployment Program or Knox MSP Program, you can skip to one of these pages (both of these procedures include enrollment in the Knox Partner Program):

  • Enroll in the Knox Deployment Program
  • Enroll in the Knox MSP Program

Enroll in the Knox Partner Program

You can apply to become a Knox partner with either a new or an existing Samsung account, provided the existing account isn’t being used with any other Knox cloud services.

  1. On the Knox Partner Program page, click BECOME A PARTNER.

  2. Sign up for a Samsung account using your business work email address and click CREATE MY ACCOUNT. Personal email addresses, such as Gmail and Hotmail, aren’t accepted.

    If you already have a Samsung account linked with this email address, sign in with your credentials, then skip to step 7.

  3. Review the listed Samsung Service Terms and Conditions, Special terms, and Notice of Financial Incentives.

    • If you want to receive optional customized content, personalized ads, and notifications with news about Samsung and its partners’ products, select the relevant checkboxes. You can unsubscribe at any time in your Samsung account settings.

    Once you’ve configured your privacy settings, click Agree.

  4. Enter the account details, then click Next to submit your Samsung account registration request.

The required account details may differ depending on your current region.

  1. Once your request is submitted, the page prompts you for a verification code, which is sent to the email address you provided. Enter the Verification code provided, then click Next.

  1. On the following page, click Done.

  2. Verify your name and work email address are correct, then click SET UP TWO-STEP VERIFICATION. To enable this security feature, you’ll be redirected to your Samsung account page. See Enable two-step verification for more details. Once you’re done, click NEXT: VERIFICATION.

  1. Enter your Registered company name and D-U-N-S Number. If you don’t know your D-U-N-S Number, or don’t know if you have one, see D-U-N-S Number. Once you’ve entered your organizations D-U-N-S Number, click NEXT: COMPANY INFO.

  1. Provide the company information used with the Knox partner account. Note that some fields may differ depending on your current region and may be automatically filled based on the information retrieved from your D-U-N-S identity, then click NEXT: AGREEMENT.

  1. Review the Knox Partner Program agreements and select whether or not you’d like to receive partner-related emails. Then, click Agree.

The agreements may differ depending on your current region.

Once your Knox partner account application is approved by the Knox team, you’ll be notified by email that your account application has been approved. You can then sign in to your Knox partner account on the Knox Partner Portal.

D-U-N-S Number

To join the Knox Partner Program, you must be a registered business entity with a valid Dun & Bradstreet D-U-N-S Number. A D-U-N-S Number is a unique nine-digit identifier that is used to access business information. You will be asked to provide your D-U-N-S number so the identity of your company can be verified.

To find your companies D-U-N-S Number, sign in to your Knox Partner Program account, then click Find your D-U-N-S Number.

Add team members to your partner account

After you’ve enrolled in the Knox Partner Program on behalf of your company, you can add other users to your account.

On the Knox Partner Portal, go to Account > User Management. From there you can click INVITE A NEW USER to send another user an email invitation containing a link to register as a user under your company.

If you’re an MSP or reseller partner, the User management page won’t be available to you in the Knox Partner Portal. Instead, use the management features in the Knox MSP Portal or Reseller Portal.

Edit your profile

Click your account icon, then select My account.

On this page, you can change settings such as your account information, identity provider settings, and privacy settings.

For detailed information about identity provider settings, see Configure identity provider settings.

Enroll in the Knox Partner Program

Step 1: Create a Samsung account for Business and register your company

You can apply to become a Knox partner with Samsung account for Business.

  1. On the Knox Partner Program page, click BECOME A PARTNER. The Sign up for Samsung account for Business page opens.

  2. Click Sign up. You’re redirected to the Create a Samsung account for Business page.

  3. Enter your company information and click Next:

    • Company name — Ensure that the company name marches the name registered with the D-U-N-S number.
    • Company country or region
    • Company website (Optional) — The website you provide is used to set up your default domain.

  4. Provide information for your admin profile:

    • First name
    • Last name
    • Work email
    • Country code and Phone number — The country code must be for the company’s country or region specified in the previous page.
    • Password
    • Confirm password

  5. Review and agree to the Privacy Notice and click Next. The Verify your identity page opens, where you can verify your work email and phone number.

  6. Enter the verification code received on your work email and click Verify. Your phone number is displayed for verification.

    • If the verification fails five times, the account is not created.
    • If the domain in your email address is already in use, an error notification displays. You can pause the sign-up process and request an account from your admin instead. If you choose to continue with the sign-up process and request an account with the duplicate domain, you’ll not be able to verify and secure the duplicate domain later.

  7. Click Send code to send a verification code to your phone number. The code is valid for three minutes only.

    • If required, extend the code expiration time by clicking Extend. You can extend the code expiration time twice.
    • If you’d like to use a different phone number for your account, you can change it by clicking Change phone number and providing the new number.

  8. Enter the verification code received by SMS and click Verify.

    If the domain in your email address is already in use, an error notification displays. You can pause the sign-up process and request an account from your admin instead.

  9. On the Create a Samsung account for Business page, review and select the Terms and Conditions and the Data processing agreement, and click Agree. Your account is created and an email notification is sent to your work email to register your company with Samsung.

  10. On the Register your company dialog, provide the following information and click Next:

  11. (Optional) If you don’t have a D-U-N-S number, select My company doesn’t have a D-U-N-S number and provide a valid VAT number or business registration number, depending on your country, instead to retrieve company details.

  12. Click Next. The Review company details dialog shows company information, such as company name, country or region, address, phone number, and website, retrieved using your D-U-N-S or VAT number.

  13. Verify and update the company information, if required, and click Next. The Provide contact for verification dialog displays.

  14. Provide details of a company representative who can verify your company, and click Submit:

    • First name
    • Last name
    • Email
    • Department
    • Job title

    Your registration is submitted. A Samsung representative will process and verify your company information. You’ll receive an email once your company is approved for Knox services.

  15. Click Next. In the next step, you’ll need to confirm your company’s local address for Samsung Knox.

Step 2: Select a Partner type and confirm company details for Knox Partner Program

You must select a Partner type and verify your company details to process and approve a Knox Partner Program account.

  1. Select one or more partner types from the Partner type drop down menu.

  2. View and verify your company details:

    • To use the company information specified in Step 1 while registering the company, switch on the Use the company address from Samsung account for Business for my application toggle.
    • To change company information, specify the updated details in the fields shown.

  3. Click Next. The Knox Partner Program agreements page displays.

Step 3: Review agreements

On the Knox Partner Program agreements page, review the agreements shown to you, and click Agree.

Your Knox Partner Program application is submitted for review and approval. You’ll receive email notifications when your company is registered with Samsung, your Knox Partner Program application is approved, and each of your selected partner types are approved. You can then access and set up the Knox Partner Portal for your company.

Add team members to your partner account

After you’ve enrolled in the Knox Partner Program on behalf of your company, you can add other users to your account. See Manage admins for details and step-by-step instructions.

If you didn’t sign in using Samsung account for Business, go to Account > User Management. From there, you can click INVITE A NEW USER to send another user an email invitation containing a link to register as a user under your company.

If you’re an MSP or reseller partner, the User management page won’t be available to you in the Knox Partner Portal. Instead, use the management features in the Knox MSP Portal or Reseller Portal.
