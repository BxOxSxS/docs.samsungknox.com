Knox Validated Program

  • 22.12
  • 23.03 UAT

The Knox Validated Program is Samsung's verification and authentication program for Enterprise Mobility Management (EMM/MDM/UEM) solution partners who meet Samsung's Knox platform and product requirements. Any EMM partners enrolled in the Knox Partner Program are eligible to apply for the Knox Validated Program. Once your company is validated, we post your solution on our global website to endorse your solution officially.

This feature is available only to EMM partners.

Request validation

Request validation by one of these methods:

  • If you're a new EMM partner who hasn't been validated before, request validation through the Knox Partner Program dashboard.

    From the dashboard, click Promote solutions > Knox Validated and then fill in the form and submit it.

    Samsung checks whether your solution meets the program requirements, and if it does, provides you with a Knox Validated Program badge, and adds the badge to your solution brief if you have one.

  • If you've been validated previously (the validation requirements are updated each year, so you may need to meet new requirements), a partner manager will reach out to you for revalidation.

Onboarding and validation flow

For more information, see Knox Validated Program for EMM solutions.
