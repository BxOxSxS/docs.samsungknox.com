Submit a support ticket

Submit a support ticket if you have any MSP-related questions or encounter any issues.

  1. Sign in to the Knox Partner Portal.
  2. Go to Support.
  3. Click Submit/View my support tickets.

What to provide

To resolve your issue as fast as possible, be prepared to collect the following information:

From the Knox MSP Portal:

  • MSP ID — From your MSP dashboard, click your account icon.

From various settings on your device:

  • About phone
    • Android version
    • Build number
    • Kernel version
  • SIM card status
    • Mobile network state
    • Signal strength
  • Wi-Fi
    • Status
    • Signal strength

From your device log:

  • Get the dumpstate log

Submit a support ticket

Submit a support ticket if you have any MSP-related questions or encounter any issues.

You can submit a support ticket from the MSP Portal or the Knox Partner Portal.

Submit a ticket from the MSP Portal

Click in the top right corner of the portal, then under Customer support, click Submit/View support tickets.

Submit a ticket from the Knox Partner Portal

  1. Sign in to the Knox Partner Portal.
  2. Go to Support.
  3. Click Submit/View my support tickets.

What to provide

To resolve your issue as quickly as possible, be prepared to collect the following information:

From the Knox MSP Portal:

  • MSP ID — From your MSP dashboard, click your account icon.

From various settings on your device:

  • About phone
    • Android version
    • Build number
    • Kernel version
  • SIM card status
    • Mobile network state
    • Signal strength
  • Wi-Fi
    • Status
    • Signal strength

From your device log:

  • Get the dumpstate log

View your support tickets

You can view your support tickets in the Knox Partner Portal as well as from the Dashboard in the Open support tickets widget. See Check your personal dashboard for details.

Access support resources

The MSP Portal includes links to this documentation as well as release updates, how-to videos, FAQs, and knowledge base articles. Support resources can be found under Resources when you click in the top right corner of the portal.
