Submit a claim

You can raise a claim request using either a phone call or email. The process for submitting a claim is as follows:

  1. Contact us using one of the following methods:
    • To call, use 1800 307 267864
    • To email, use B2B.Care@samsung.com
  2. Include the following information in your message:
    • Device IMEI
    • Alternative contact information. This is an email address and mobile number that isn’t linked to the device or registered when the plan was purchased.
    • Nature of the issue with the device.
  3. Based on your plan’s date of purchase, you may need to make a payment as follows:
    • If the plan was purchased before July 24th, 2024, then after the request is successfully raised, you’ll receive a deductible payment link on both your registered email address, provided at the time of plan purchase, and your alternate email address.
    • If the plan was purchased after July 24th, 2024, no additional payment is required.
  4. If applicable to you, pay the the one-time deductible using the provided link.
  5. Call or email the dedicated support team and provide the following information:
    • Your Claim ID.
    • If applicable, a confirmation of payment.
    • Your service preference:
      • Self-visit to a service center.
      • Pick-up and drop-off service from your location.

The device is submitted for repair based on the service mode you selected. After the device is repaired, it’ll be delivered back to you.
