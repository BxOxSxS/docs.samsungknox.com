Claim guide—Malaysia

Submit a claim

  1. You can submit a claim request using one of the following methods:
    • Log into my.careplus.co/portal/login and raise a claim yourself.
    • Call on the support line +603-9078 4999.
    • Email cs.my@careplus.co.
    • Chat live at my.careplus.co.
  2. Provide product details such as IMEI/SN number and explain the nature of damage/issue.
    NOTE—If the device is declared Beyond Economic Repair (BER), a replacement service fee is required from the customer. The payment details are sent to the customer's registered contact details.
  3. Choose one of the following convenient service modes:
    • Self drop-off your device at an authorized Samsung Service Center and submit the device for repair.
    • Schedule a pick-up & drop-off of the device for repair.

Service features

  • Free pick-up and delivery service throughout Malaysia.
  • No service fee for repair.

Terms and Conditions for service request

  • Device must be registered on an active Samsung Care+ for Business license.
  • Device must experience unintentional physical and liquid breakage. This includes any unforeseen and unintentional physical damage that occurs due to handling errors, liquid or external events at a defined place and time, which stops the device from working normally.
  • Breakage or liquid breakage service requests for device repairs or replacement are provided up to the Maximum Repair Value (for Breakage & Liquid/Water Damage) as stipulated in Summary of Your Details section in the Terms of Service of your Samsung Care+ for Business contract.
  • Breakdown service requests for repairs or replacement of device up to the Maximum Repair Value (for Breakdown) upon expiration of Manufacturer’s Warranty for a term of 1 year or 2 years (whichever is applicable) as stipulated in Summary of Your Details section in the Terms of Service of your Samsung Care+ for Business contract.
NOTE—Service request will not be entertained if:
  • The Samsung Care+ for Business Repair Contract has expired.
  • Device is not registered on an active Samsung Care+ for Business license.
  • It involves theft and loss of device.
NOTE—For complete details of the services available and the applicable exclusions under your Samsung Care+, please refer to the relevant Terms of Service of your Samsung Care+ for Business Repair Contract.
