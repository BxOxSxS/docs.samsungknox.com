Introduction to special edition

Last updated October 2nd, 2024

The following information only applies to users in the US region.

Special edition devices are purchased in bulk and bundled with Knox license keys. The following two special edition device bundles are available for US customers:

  • Verizon Enterprise Edition — includes Samsung Galaxy S24 devices, a three-year Knox Suite license, and standard one-year manufacturer’s warranty.
  • Galaxy Healthcare Edition — includes Samsung Galaxy A15 devices, a two-year Knox E-FOTA license, a two-year Knox Configure Dynamic Edition license, standard one-year manufacturer’s warranty, and a three-year product lifecycle.
