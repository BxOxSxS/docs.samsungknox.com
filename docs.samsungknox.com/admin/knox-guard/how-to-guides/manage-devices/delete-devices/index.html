Delete devices

Last updated November 19th, 2025

Consider removing a device from Knox Guard if it’s rejected or payment complete. When a device is deleted from Knox Guard, the device is removed from the server and the Knox Guard client is uninstalled. Once deleted, all of the device’s information and history is removed.

Deleting a device only removes it from Knox Guard. If you use any other Knox cloud service, you’ll need to navigate to the Knox Admin Portal and delete the device from the common device list to completely remove it from your tenant.

Delete selected devices

To permanently delete a device from Knox Guard:

  1. On the Devices page, select one or more devices.

  2. Click ACTIONS > Delete device.

    The Delete device action is only available in the actions menu if the selected device is in the Pending, Rejected, Activating, Resetting, Completing, or Complete device state.

  3. Click DELETE.

  4. (Optional) You can click ACTIONS > View device deletion log to review a log of the devices removed from the Knox Guard console.

    Refer to the following device log information:

    • DEVICE — IMEI or serial number of the deleted device.
    • DEVICE MODEL — Model of the deleted device.
    • DATE DELETED — Date the device was deleted.
    • DELETED BY — The user that deleted the device.

To save the device log, click DOWNLOAD DEVICES AS CSV.

Delete devices in bulk

You can delete devices in bulk by entering their IMEIs or serial numbers into a standardized template. The maximum number of devices you can delete in one action is 10,000.

You can only delete devices in the Pending, Rejected, Activating, Resetting, Completing, or Complete device state.

To delete devices in bulk:

  1. Click BULK ACTIONS in the bottom of the left navigation pane.

  2. To download a standard template, click Download CSV template.

    For more information on how to create a properly formatted CSV file, click Instructions for all bulk actions from the bottom left-hand side of the Bulk actions screen.

  3. Under DEVICE MANAGEMENT, click DELETE DEVICES and upload your CSV file containing the device IDs.

  4. Click CONFIRM.

Re-upload devices

If you’ve accidentally deleted a device from the Knox Guard console, you have two options to get the device back:

  • Re-upload the device to Knox Guard using a CSV file.

  • Ask your reseller to re-upload the device to your account.

    If you use any other Knox cloud service, delete the device from all your services before re-uploading it into your account.

For more information, see Re-register devices into Knox Guard.
