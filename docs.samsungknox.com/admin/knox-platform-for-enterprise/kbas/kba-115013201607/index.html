How to automatically enable email sync while roaming

Last updated May 6th, 2025

Categories:

Device management

Environment

  • Knox Platform for Enterprise
  • Samsung Email

Overview

This article guides you through how to enable automatic data syncing in Samsung Email while your device is roaming.

How to enable automatic email sync while roaming

To enable automatic email sync while roaming:

  1. Open the Samsung Email app.

  2. In the top left corner, tap (Menu icon) > (Settings icon) and select the required email account.

  3. Tap Email sync schedule. The Sync schedule page opens.

  4. Enable Peak schedule.

  5. Set Sync schedule while roaming to Using regular schedule.

You can now sync emails automatically while your device is roaming.

The user interface examples in this article may differ depending on the current version of your device.
