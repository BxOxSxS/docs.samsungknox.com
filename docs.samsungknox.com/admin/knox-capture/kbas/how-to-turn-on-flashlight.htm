How to turn on device flashlight with Knox Capture scanner active

Environment

  • Knox Capture

Overview

If the device user is using the Knox Capture scanner in dark conditions, they may want to turn on the device's flashlight. To do so, you need to enable the flashlight button in the device's Knox Capture profile.

How do I turn on my device's flashlight when using the Knox Capture scanner?

To see the flashlight button in the scanner UI, you must first ensure the device's Knox Capture profile has the Flashlight button feature enabled in the scan engine settings.

Then, trigger the camera scanner, and tap the flashlight icon in the upper-left corner of the screen to turn the flashlight on or off.

Additional information

For more information on how to configure the Knox Capture scanner, see Scan engine settings.
