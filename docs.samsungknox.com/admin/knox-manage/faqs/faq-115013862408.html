Faq 115013862408

Last updated July 26th, 2023

Does Samsung access and transfer any personal information stored on Knox Manage enabled devices?

No, Samsung does not access and transfer any personal information on both Knox Manage servers (located in US, EU, and Asia) and Knox Manage enabled devices. If you require technical support, you can choose to grant access to your Admin Console in order for support engineers to better understand the issue.
